General information

With Doom We Come is the eigth full-length album by Summoning. It was release in 2018 after a 5 year hiatus.

Illustration

The art in the booklet was done by Clarkson Stanfield (1793-1867), Alexandre Calame (1810-1864), and Edward Theodore Compton (1849-1921). The dragon painting on the cover was done by Georg Janny (1864-1935).
